> [!spellbook-spell-block] Share Memory
> **School:** divination; **Level** bard 2, sorcerer/wizard 2, witch 2, occultist 2, psychic 2, mesmerist 2
> **Casting Time** 1 standard action  
> **Components** V, S  
> **Range** touch
